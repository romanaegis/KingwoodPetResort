LUXURY CARE SINCE 1979

A sanctuary where your pets truly belong.

Exquisite boutique pet boarding with large climate control suites, private outdoor runs, 24-hour web access, and expert grooming in a lush, peaceful environment designed for ultimate comfort.

We started Kingwood Kennels with 10 kennels. We are no longer just a kennel; we are a pet resort with 30 indoor and outdoor runs, 10 luxury suites, and 24-hour webcam access for pet owners. Everything is climate-controlled and designed for pet safety and comfort.

Established in 1979, we were the very first pet care facility in Kingwood

More than a resort, we are a Sanctuary built on decades of passion and five acres of land

At Kingwood pet resort, we believe every pet deserves a vacation as refined as their owners. Founded with the vision of blending rustic charm with high-end care, we’ve spent years perfecting the balance between professional supervision and heartfelt affection. Every pet has a private space free from any bothersome neighbors

Our facility is meticulously maintained to provide a safe, sanitary, flea-free, and serene environment, from our spacious exercise fields, climate-controlled boarding suites, to our 24-hour webcam access. Every inch of Kingwood Pet Resort is curated for pet Wellness and owner Peace of Mind
